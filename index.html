WHAT IS UNIT CELL?

Unit cell is defined as the smallest unit of a crystal lattice which on repetition in all direction leads to development of a lattice crystal lattice

types of unit cells

  1. Primitive unit cell

    2. When constituent particles are present at corners of a unit cell, it is called a primitive unit cell

  2. Centred unit cell

    3. When constituent particles are present at other position other than the corner of a lattice the unit cell is known as centered unit cell. they are of three type

    1. Body-centered unit cell- when constituent particle are present at body centre other than corner of a unit cell it is known as body centered unit cell
    2. Face centered unit cell- when constituent particles are present at the faces of a unit cell other than at the corner of unit cell. it is known as face centered unit cell
    3. end Centered unit cell- when constituent particles are present at any one of the two opposite faces oither than the corner such a unit cell is known as end centered unit cell
